Research Questions

Here is a list of research questions I find interesting. Broadly, these questions involve a combination of computation, cognitive science, and linguistics. If you're interested in collaborating or have thoughts on these problems, shoot me an email at nicholas_tomlin@brown.edu.
  1. How can computational linguistics benefit from modeling hierarchical structure? Are TreeRNNs a viable solution? How should we define our parse trees, and can they be learned in tandem with the semantics?
  2. How should we structure meaning representations? Can meaning be learned functionally, e.g., through reinforcement learning?
  3. How does sentence meaning contribute to knowledge representation? One idea is to define the world in terms of a POMDP, and use language to update the belief state. But then how do we represent possible world states (i.e., latent states of the POMDP)?
