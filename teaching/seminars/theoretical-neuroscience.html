Foundations of Theoretical Neurocience

In Spring 2020, I'll be organizing a student seminar on the foundations of theoretical neuroscience. We'll begin by following the Dayan & Abbott (2003) textbook, and then progress to papers and other resources. This is loosely inspired by the MIT BCS Philosophy Circle.

Week 1: Introduction to Model Neurons

Week 6: Network Models II
