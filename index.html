Nicholas Tomlin

About Me

I'm a first-year PhD student at UC Berkeley. I'm affiliated with BAIR and Berkeley NLP, as well as the UCSF Chang Lab. I'm broadly interested in computational linguistics, theoretical neuroscience, reinforcement learning, and cognitive science. My advisor is Dan Klein.

Before coming to Berkeley, I studied mathematics and linguistics at Brown University, where I was advised by Ellie Pavlick.
