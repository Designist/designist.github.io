About Me

I'm a second-year PhD student at UC Berkeley. I'm affiliated with BAIR and Berkeley NLP, as well as the UCSF Chang Lab. I'm broadly interested in computational linguistics, theoretical neuroscience, reinforcement learning, and cognitive science. My advisor is Dan Klein. As of Summer 2020, I am supported by the NSF Graduate Research Fellowship. Before coming to Berkeley, I studied math and linguistics at Brown University, where I was advised by Ellie Pavlick.

As of Fall 2020, I'm organizing the Berkeley NLP Seminar. Please contact me at if you would like to give a (virtual) talk. This semester, I'm also holding open advising hours for students at Berkeley.
